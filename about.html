A Bit About Me

I am 26 years old, and a graphic design student at Conestoga College almost finished year two of three for an advanced diploma. I am eager to see what the future holds. I exhibit basic capabilities, like being a fast learner, customer service and cashier experience, organizational expertise, working well alone or on a team, and a great work ethic.

I understand Adobe applications such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and more, and experience creating brand identity, storyboarding, animation, package design, etc. With plenty of practice in time management and a passion for designing pieces that encourage people not only to admire but to stop and think. I also have practice working with actual companies, such as ‘Helping Hands’ in the non-profit Creative Day for Social Good, with redesigning their website; a charity group that helps youths to get their volunteer hours they need in High School so they can graduate.
